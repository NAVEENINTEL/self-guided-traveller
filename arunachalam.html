Overview

New York City is a vibrant, cosmopolitan destination that offers something for everyone. From iconic landmarks like the Statue of Liberty and Central Park to world-class museums and shopping districts, there is always something to see and do in the city that never sleeps.

Our self-guided tour of New York City includes a detailed itinerary and map, as well as recommendations for things to see and do in each neighborhood. You'll have the freedom to explore the city at your own pace, with the security of knowing that you have a reliable plan to follow.
